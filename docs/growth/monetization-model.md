# Monetization Model (MVP to Post-MVP)

## 1) Revenue streams

### Stream A - Agent lead products (MVP+)
- Qualified lead routing and performance reporting.
- Pricing options: subscription tier or lead-credit model.

### Stream B - Promoted listings (Post-MVP)
- Sponsored visibility placements with relevance guardrails.
- Must preserve clear ad labeling and user trust.

### Stream C - Partner marketplace referrals (Post-MVP)
- Mortgage/insurance/service referral partnerships with consent controls.

## 2) Pricing principles

- Align cost to measurable value (lead quality, response, conversion).
- Offer transparent pricing with performance dashboards.
- Prevent pay-to-win ranking behavior that harms organic relevance.

## 3) Guardrails

- Sponsored exposure cannot break baseline relevance quality.
- Compliance checks required for partner referral disclosures.
- Revenue experimentation must include user trust metrics.

## 4) Launch recommendation

Start with agent subscription + lead quality analytics before introducing sponsored inventory products.
