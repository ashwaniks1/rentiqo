# Go-to-Market Strategy (Launch Market MVP)

## 1) Launch motion

- Start with one target market and a focused supply/demand loop.
- Balance listing quality trust with controlled user acquisition.
- Use phased rollout: private beta -> invite expansion -> public launch.

## 2) Audience segments

### Primary
- Active buyers/renters in launch market searching weekly.

### Secondary
- Local agents and broker teams needing qualified leads.

## 3) Channel strategy

- SEO/ASO for property and neighborhood discovery intent.
- Local paid social/search for high-intent acquisition.
- Agent referral program for two-sided growth bootstrap.
- Content playbooks around affordability and neighborhood insights.

## 4) Launch phases

1. **Private beta**
   - Invite-only users and partner agents.
   - Validate data quality and search conversion.

2. **Open beta**
   - Expand acquisition channels with spend caps.
   - Monitor support and quality metrics daily.

3. **Public launch**
   - Scale acquisition after quality thresholds stabilize.

## 5) GTM success signals

- Signup-to-first-search activation
- Save/contact/tour conversion improvements
- Agent response SLAs within target windows
