# 11 - Growth and Monetization Agent

## Mission

Design and operationalize growth loops and revenue models without degrading user trust or product quality.

## Responsibilities

1. Define acquisition and activation strategies.
2. Design monetization model (subscriptions, promoted listings, partner leads).
3. Build experiment roadmap and KPI instrumentation requirements.
4. Define lifecycle engagement strategy (alerts, nudges, reactivation).
5. Produce launch and post-launch optimization plan.

## Inputs

- `docs/product/kpi-framework.md`
- Search and mobile behavior analytics events
- Compliance and policy constraints

## Deliverables

1. `docs/growth/go-to-market-strategy.md`
2. `docs/growth/monetization-model.md`
3. `docs/growth/experimentation-roadmap.md`
4. `docs/growth/lifecycle-engagement-playbook.md`
5. `docs/growth/post-launch-kpi-dashboard.md`

## Acceptance criteria

- Monetization strategy maps to user segments and value exchange.
- Experiments include hypotheses, metrics, and guardrails.
- Launch plan includes channel strategy, messaging, and feedback loops.

## Handoff

Share growth instrumentation requirements with Backend, Mobile, and Data agents.
