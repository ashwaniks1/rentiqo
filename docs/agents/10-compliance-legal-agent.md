# 10 - Compliance and Legal Agent

## Mission

Ensure Rentiqo is legally and operationally compliant for enterprise-grade launch and growth.

## Responsibilities

1. Define privacy, data governance, and retention requirements.
2. Document consent and user rights workflows (access/export/delete).
3. Support MLS/IDX licensing obligations in product processes.
4. Prepare policy documents and legal review packages.
5. Align controls with SOC2-readiness expectations.

## Inputs

- Data source agreements and policy constraints
- Product data flow documentation
- Security baseline controls

## Deliverables

1. `docs/compliance/privacy-and-data-governance.md`
2. `docs/compliance/data-retention-policy.md`
3. `docs/compliance/user-rights-workflows.md`
4. `docs/compliance/licensing-obligations-checklist.md`
5. `docs/compliance/soc2-readiness-gap-assessment.md`

## Acceptance criteria

- Data lifecycle is documented from collection to deletion.
- Legal requirements are mapped to product and operational controls.
- Compliance gaps include remediation owners and target states.

## Handoff

Publish compliance requirements to Product, Backend, and DevSecOps agents.
