# Rentiqo Personas and Jobs-to-be-Done

## Persona 1: Buyer (Primary)

## Profile
- First-time or repeat home buyer.
- Time-constrained and comparison-heavy behavior.

## Jobs-to-be-done
1. When I browse homes, help me quickly narrow options in my target location and budget.
2. When I evaluate a listing, help me trust whether it is still relevant and fairly priced.
3. When I find a home I like, let me act immediately (save, alert, contact, schedule).

## Pain points
- Stale listing information.
- Too many tabs/apps to compare options.
- Slow responses from agents.

## Persona 2: Renter (Secondary)

## Profile
- Often searches under tighter timelines than buyers.
- Highly sensitive to monthly affordability and commute.

## Jobs-to-be-done
1. Let me find available homes that match my move-in timeline.
2. Let me compare rent, fees, and commute quickly.
3. Let me receive immediate updates when relevant inventory changes.

## Pain points
- Inconsistent availability status.
- Hidden fees discovered late.
- Low signal-to-noise search results.

## Persona 3: Agent/Broker

## Profile
- Individual or small-team professional serving local market demand.

## Jobs-to-be-done
1. Give me qualified, intent-rich leads.
2. Help me respond quickly and schedule tours efficiently.
3. Show me listing/lead performance clearly.

## Pain points
- Low-quality leads.
- Fragmented lead communication.
- Poor analytics visibility on performance.

## Persona 4: Admin/Operations

## Profile
- Internal team responsible for trust, support, and data quality.

## Jobs-to-be-done
1. Let me detect stale, duplicate, or suspicious listings quickly.
2. Let me resolve user and listing issues with full context.
3. Let me monitor platform health and policy compliance.

## Pain points
- Lack of unified moderation tooling.
- Manual triage overhead.
- Limited auditability.

## Priority mapping to MVP

- Buyer: Critical
- Renter: High
- Agent/Broker: High
- Admin/Operations: High
